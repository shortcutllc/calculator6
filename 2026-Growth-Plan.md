## Slide 11: Lead Estimation Methodology

### Calculation Variables

| Variable | Assumption |
|----------|------------|
| Attendees in target persona | 30-50% of total (HR, Benefits, Workplace Exp) |
| Booth traffic rate | 10-15% of relevant attendees visit a given booth |
| Conversation-to-lead conversion | 50-70% of booth conversations = qualified lead |
| Speaking/session bonus | +20-30% leads if you have a speaking slot |

### Example: Workhuman (3,000 attendees)

- Target personas: ~60% = 1,800 relevant
- Booth traffic: 10% = 180 visits
- Qualified: 60% = ~108 leads at top end
- **Conservative (no speaking slot): 20-30 leads**

### Example: Wellbeing at Work NYC (500 attendees)

- Target personas: ~80% = 400 relevant (highly curated)
- Booth/networking capture: 15% = 60 conversations
- Qualified: 70% = ~40 leads at top end
- **Conservative: 15-25 leads**

### Lead Multipliers

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Speaking slot | +50-100% |
| Sponsored session | +30% |
| Pre-conference outreach | +20% |
| On-site activation | +25% |

## Slide 19: How It Works

**Company-level funds for group wellness events ‚Äî not individual employee spending**

### The Reimbursement Flow

1. **HR books Shortcut** ‚Üí Group wellness day for the office
2. **Shortcut invoices company** ‚Üí B2B transaction, company pays us
3. **Company submits to insurer** ‚Üí Invoice + receipts to Health Engagement team
4. **Insurer reimburses company** ‚Üí 45-60 days processing

### This IS Our Model

- **Company-level decision** ‚Äî HR/benefits team books us
- **Group service delivery** ‚Äî massage day for the office
- **B2B invoice** ‚Äî we bill the company directly
- **Pooled corporate funds** ‚Äî not individual employee accounts
- **Massage is eligible** ‚Äî confirmed as qualifying wellness activity

### This is NOT Our Model

- Individual employee LSA/HSA/FSA spending
- Employees browsing a marketplace to book
- Per-employee reimbursement or cards
- Consumer-facing transactions

**Why This Matters:** LSA platforms like Forma and Benepass are for individual employee spending ‚Äî not relevant to our B2B model. Our focus: help companies access their **insurer wellness funds** and connect with **benefits brokers** who recommend vendors.
